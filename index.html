Importance of Trees

Trees are very important in our life. They are useful for every living being on earth. Trees give greenery to the environment. They give us oxygen to breathe.

They also give us many other things like

  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Medicines
  • Paper
  • Wood

Trees are our best friends and we need to protect them. Read more about Trees Importance to get the reasons why living trees are valuable.
